Many many states

I have been to every state on the east coast from Florida to Massachussetts, but I did not go further north.

But not all of them

I went to California too, but not Hawaii, Alaska, or any other western states for that matter.

Where to now?

I think my favorite state is either California, Florida, or Pennsylvania. Pennsylvania has nice roads and mountains but is kind of boring. Florida is nice and there is lots to do and see, but it is expensive. California is probably my favorite but we will see if I ever go back, what will I think then?
